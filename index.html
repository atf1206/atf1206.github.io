Legal Experience

As a lawyer:

Patent Prosecution: Andrew is a patent attorney capable of fully prosecuting patent applications through the U.S. Patent & Trademark Office, including filing amendments, conducting examiner interviews, filing appeals, and filing continuation applications. He has drafted complete applications for original U.S. applications from scratch or based on previous foreign filings, and has prepared and filed U.S. national stage applications, international applications, and design applications. His experience includes patent applications in the areas of electrical engineering (e.g. microwave circuits), physics (e.g. satellite dishes, software artificial physics systems), mechanical engineering (e.g. solar tracking panels), materials science (e.g. quartz glass, chemical deposition), and others.

Transactional Practice: Andrew drafts memoranda regarding non-infringement or infringement for clients' products or patents, advises and performs transfers of intellectual property ownership, and advises inventors and companies on intellectual property strategy.
